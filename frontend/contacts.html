Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get started with AcademeX?

You can start using AcademeX within 24 hours of signing up. Our onboarding process is designed to get your institution up and running quickly, with full implementation support available.

Do you offer training for staff and teachers?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training sessions for administrators, teachers, and staff. This includes live webinars, video tutorials, and personalized training sessions to ensure everyone is comfortable with the platform.

What kind of support do you provide?

We offer 24/7 email support, live chat during business hours, and phone support for urgent issues. Premium customers also receive dedicated account management and priority support.

Can AcademeX integrate with our existing systems?

Absolutely! AcademeX offers robust API integration capabilities and can connect with most existing student information systems, learning management systems, and administrative software.
